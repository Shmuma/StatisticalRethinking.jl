hpdi

StatisticalRethinking.hpdi — Method

hpdi

Compute high density region.

hpdi(x; alpha)

Derived from hpd in MCMCChains.jl.

Alpha represents the 2-sided tail area, i.e. p < 0.055% and p > 0.945%.
